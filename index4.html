About Us

We are a dedicated Airline Company here to help make dreams come true. We aspire to deliver the best travel experiences to all our guests. We want you to see our beautiful country as we see it. We want to inspire young explorers.

Our company was founded in 1890, we have the second largest fleet in the world. We have won the best airline award 42 times.
